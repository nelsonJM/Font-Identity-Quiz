What's this all about?

This quiz is a personal project I built, first and foremost, as a conclusion to this JavaScript study course. It's also meant to help me study and identify typefaces.

"Can you identify fonts?" I have been asked this question several times over the last year.

I decided to build a simple quiz that would focus my attention on the anatomy of a particular font. I've tried other tools and methods, but they all seemed to focus either strictly on typeface anatomy in general (counter, terminal, crossbar, etc.) or words set in a particular font that I needed to identify (similar to this quiz).

I hope to set this quiz apart by drawing attention to the specific anatomy of a particular typeface and providing additional information (when available) about that anatomy.
