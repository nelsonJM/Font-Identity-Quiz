What's this all about?

I built this quiz shortly after completing this JavaScript study course. It's purpose is twofold:

  1. A practical application of JavaScript
  2. An opportunity to study fonts/typefaces/letters wearing cool suits

"Can you identify fonts?"

Somebody asked me this some time ago. It has been stuck in my head ever since, so I made it a small project.

I decided to focus on the anatomy of particular letters of a particular font. Other quizzes seem to focus strictly on typeface anatomy in general (counter, terminal, crossbar, etc.) or words set in a particular font with no emphasis on the font's distinguishing characteristics.

I hope to set this quiz apart by drawing attention to the specific anatomy of a particular typeface and providing additional information (when available) about that anatomy.
