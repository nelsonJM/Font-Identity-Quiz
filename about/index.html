What's this all about?

I built this quiz shortly after completing this JavaScript study course. It's purpose is twofold:

  1. A practical application of JavaScript
  2. An opportunity to study fonts/typefaces/letters wearing cool suits

"Can you identify fonts?"

This a job interview question I was once asked. Silly question? Maybe. But it has been stuck in my head for some time and I thought it might make a good topic for a small project.

I decided to build a simple quiz that would focus my attention on the anatomy of a particular font. I've tried other tools and methods, but they all seemed to focus either strictly on typeface anatomy in general (counter, terminal, crossbar, etc.) or words set in a particular font that I needed to identify (similar to this quiz).
